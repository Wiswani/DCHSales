Who We Are

Dean C. Henke, AHC
Dean started DCH Sales, LLC in 2012, an independent manufacturers sales representative company for architectural doors and hardware.
Dean has over 35 years in the door and hardware industry working for two different distributors. He has seen the industry change. Dean’s desire is to promote and sell products from America. Dean calls on end users, distributors and architects.
Personally, Dean, along with his wife Pamela, enjoy travel and golfing. With their three sons, Dean enjoys hunting, fishing, golf and curling during the long Wisconsin winters.
deanthedoorman@gmail.com

Eric J. Markham
Eric partnered with DCH Sales, LLC in 2015. Eric has 20 years of business development experience. His background includes operations management, marketing, and client relations. He is currently continuing his education through the Door and Hardware Institute.
When he is not on the road meeting with clients, Eric enjoys spending time with his wife Tammy, watching his daughter Olivia dance, and his son Carter participate in athletics. Eric’s hobbies are hunting and golf.
eric@dchsalesteam.com 

DCH Sales, LLC is an independent manufacturers sales representative company for architectural doors and hardware with over 30 years of experience in the door and hardware industry. The companies we represent are great companies that stand behind their products which are crafted with pride in the USA and Canada. Knowledgeable staff is available to provide excellent customer service.

Dean and Eric have extensive experience with these companies, their personnel, facilities and products from their time in the industry and working with three distributors. More product lines will be added in the future. As our industry continues to import more products from overseas, the DCH sales team will work hard to represent truly “Made-in-America” products.

By remaining Independent Sales Representatives, it allows Dean and Eric the freedom to choose what manufacturers to partner with. As more product lines are added to their portfolio, you can trust that they will be products from manufacturers of the highest quality, just like those we currently represent.
